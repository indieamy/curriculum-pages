    • AVID Critical Thinking and Engagement: A Schoolwide Approach

    AVID Critical Thinking and Engagement: A Schoolwide Approach is designed to help teachers schoolwide integrate high-engagement college-preparedness strategies into their instruction. The tools within this book, along with the online resources, will support teachers as they build relational capacity within their classrooms, and cultivate metacognition, collaboration, inquiry, and leadership skills to better prepare all students for success in their classes and beyond.
